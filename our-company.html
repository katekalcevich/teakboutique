Our Company

Teak Boutique Inc. is a Canadian company located in Toronto, Ontario. Teak Boutique is owned by Peter Kalcevic, a former cottage owner who recognized the need for high quality teak outdoor furniture in Ontario.

We can help you furnish your outdoor living spaces with beautiful, comfortable and durable teak furniture.

Furniture delivery is available. We can deliver our products to your home fully assembled, sanded, finished and ready to use.

Quality Guarantee

All of our furniture is Grade A teak manufactured by skilled crafts people in Indonesia. The wood is kiln dried and each piece of furniture is constructed with mortise and tenon joinery.

Teak Boutique guarantees that our teak garden furniture sold for residential will be free from defects in materials and workmanship for 2 years. All hardware attached to furniture is guaranteed as well. Replacement hardware may be substituted if the original hardware is not available.

The purchase of teak furniture is an investment in quality. When used as outdoor furniture, teak can last 75 years.
