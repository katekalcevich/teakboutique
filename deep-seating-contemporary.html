Deep Seating: Contemporary

Algarve

The Algarve collection is inspired by classic Danish modern design, with clean lines, sharp angles and balanced proportions. Pieces are interpreted in premium solid teak to ensure years of enjoyment.
Algarve


Amalfi

Distinguished by angular seats, square frames, and tapered legs, Amalfi is a modern classic–and a specification favorite of architects and interior designers. Constructed from premium solid teak.
Amalfi


Azores

Azores strikes the perfect balance between transitional and modern design. Angular planes of hand woven fiber are supported by a sturdy base of solid teak hardwood. All frames are constructed from all-weather wicker fiber that has been wrapped around an aluminum frame, and the finest Grade A teak wood.
Azores


Culebra

The design of our Culebra collection recalls the windswept beauty of the Spanish Caribbean, with flowing lines and our textured charcoal wicker that simulates vintage, weathered furniture.
Culebra


Frances

The chic, sloped arm styling of Frances is relaxed yet sophisticated. Seating pieces are deep and comfortable with plush, luxurious cushions. Frames are constructed from only the finest all-weather wicker and sturdy bases of solid teak hardwood.
Frances


Hana

Hana Deep Seating intertwines modern aesthetics with timeless craftsmanship. Solid Grade A teak frames are complemented by the contrasting texture of our Sisal all-weather wicker. Enhanced by sling seat decks beneath the cushion, inviting moments of sheer relaxation.
Hana


Jupiter

Our Jupiter collection consists of primarily woven frames that showcase a modern, angular design. The open architecture of the seating reveals a floating seat deck that supports thick, comfortable cushions. The distinctive “X” motif within the arms is made from solid teak wood; this design element is echoed in the matching wicker tables. When exposed to the elements, the brown teak will weather to a handsome silver-gray that complements the wicker fiber.
Jupiter


Milano

Milano reinvents classic wicker styling with a modern upholstered seating surface, eliminating the need for a cushion. Seats are gently padded with reticulated (flow through) foam that drains quickly when wet. Upholstered surfaces are crafted from Batyline™, a 100% vinyl-coated polyester textile that is durable, fade resistant and easy to care for.
Milano


Montauk

Montauk is a comfortable lounge collections, with angled backrests, plush cushions, and extra deep seats that have flexible sling decking. Frames are crafted from solid distressed teak wood that showcases natural characteristics in the timber, such as knots, checking, and other irregularities. All pieces are hand-painted in a Vintage finish for a weathered appearance.
Montauk


Ojai

With hints of retro styling, the Ojai collection is reminiscent of old California. Pieces are relaxed yet modern, slightly low to the ground, and incredibly comfortable. Ojai is crafted from all-weather wicker that is hand-woven around rustproof aluminum frames.
Ojai


St. Barts

Our St. Barts collection has a modern, livable style that assimilates to countless settings. Pieces are crafted from all-weather rope that has the natural look of authentic hemp rope. Frames are substantial and made from rustproof aluminum with solid teak wood feet.
St. Barts


Westport

The Westport sectional is elegant in its simplicity and versatility. A limitless range of seating configurations can be made using varying combinations of armless chairs and corner/side units. Recessed teak plinths give each unit a floating appearance, and all pieces are hand-woven with the finest all-weather wicker around a powder coated aluminum frame, ensuring years of enjoyment. Stainless steel clips are provided to secure seating units together.
Westport
