Welcome

Teak Boutique is your source for quality teak furniture in Canada — delivered to your home assembled, sanded and ready to use.

We only sell Grade A teak furniture. Grade B comes from teak trees less than 15 years old. Water damage will ruin the furniture in a few years.

How can you tell the difference? Lift up a corner of the furniture — Grade A teak feels very heavy.
