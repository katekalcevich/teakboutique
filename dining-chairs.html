Café

The Café collection seamlessly blends elegance with durability. Each seating piece is meticulously crafted from powder-coated aluminum and all-weather wicker, setting a sophisticated tone for patios or outdoor eateries. Dining chairs are stocked in three wicker combinations with our Rattan frame color. All seating can be customized using six wicker colors, set against either Rattan or White frames.
Café chairs


Catherine

The Catherine collection uses a classic blend of mixed materials for a relaxed, elegant aesthetic. Seating surfaces on dining and club pieces are crafted from extra-large strands of all-weather wicker, arranged in a geometric pattern, which provide just the right amount of give to eliminate the need for cushions. Choose from a solid teak frame paired with Ash wicker, or a powder-coated aluminum frame in White complemented by Ivory wicker. Alternatively, personalize the frames with additional wicker color options.
Catherine


Chelsea

The Chelsea collection has a classic transitional design that complements a broad range of architectural styles. The dining armchair has ergonomically curved slats on the seat and back for exceptional comfort when used without a cushion.
Chelsea chairs


Classic

Our collection of Classic items share a common theme: well-designed traditional furniture with touches of American Craftsman style. The Classic armchair is constructed from solid teak for exceptional quality and features a scooped seat.
Classic chairs


Hana

The Hana Dining Chairs intertwine modern aesthetics with timeless craftsmanship. Solid Grade A teak frames are complemented by the contrasting texture of our Sisal all-weather wicker. Dining chairs are beautifully contoured and offer a comfortable dining experience without the need for added cushions. Ships 2 per carton.
Hana chairs


Monterey Folding

Graceful curves, sturdy construction, and solid brass hardware are all features of our Monterey folding dining chairs. These sleek chairs complement any of our dining tables and, with the press of a thumb mechanism, easily fold for convenient storage when not in use. Available in both a side and armchair version.
Monterey folding chairs


Nantucket

Our Nantucket dining chair epitomizes all that is best in contemporary, outdoor dining furniture. With a gently curving back and angled seat, the Nantucket dining chair has been specifically designed for comfort and style, and can be used with or without cushions.
